NEW THIS WEEK: DRAW WITH WORDS

In Draw With Words, simply click and drag your mouse across the canvas to paint a picture with words! Literally! If you drag your mouse faster, the words get bigger! What kind of picture can you make with words? Try it yourself! When you're done, try drawing your own picture with words at home! Use words that describe the picture you're drawing.
